## Definitions

- **Wizard start**: User clicks past hero screen (s0 → s1)
- **Wizard completion**: User reaches venue results (s4)
- **Booking form submit**: User fills and submits the contact modal
- **Lead**: A booking form submission with valid contact info
- **Match**: A venue recommended to a customer
- **Booking confirmed**: Customer verbally/email confirms they're proceeding with a venue
- **Venue partner**: A venue that has agreed to any partnership tier (free, featured, or commission)
