# VENUE — Business Plan

> **Miami's Private Event Marketplace**
> Prepared by Georg Chimion | March 2026

---

## What Is VENUE?

VENUE is a free platform that helps people in Miami find and book private event spaces — birthday parties, corporate events, baby showers, weddings, and more. Users describe their event, and we match them with the right venue from our curated database of real Miami spaces.

**Think of it as:** The OpenTable of private events for Miami.

---

## The Problem

Planning a private event in Miami is frustrating:

- **Peerspace/Giggster** — National platforms, impersonal, no local knowledge
- **Event planners** — Charge $2,000–$10,000 just to find a space
- **Google/Yelp** — Hours of research, no way to filter by event type or budget
- **Venues themselves** — No standard pricing, different contact methods, slow responses

**Nobody owns the "find a private event space in Miami" search.**

---

## The Opportunity

- Miami private event average ticket: **$2,500–$5,000**
- Event culture + Instagram obsession = high demand for curated spaces
- Key Google searches with no dominant local player:
  - "private event space miami" — 500 searches/month
  - "baby shower venue miami" — 300/month
  - "birthday party venue brickell" — 150/month
  - "corporate event space wynwood" — 200/month

---

## How It Works

1. **User visits VENUE** → picks event type, guest count, budget, vibe
2. **We show matched venues** → real Miami spaces with pricing, capacity, amenities
3. **User requests a space** → we get the lead via email
4. **We connect user and venue** → concierge-style, manually at first

**Free for consumers. Always.**

---

### Phase 2 — Automate (Weeks 4–6) 💰 $0–$100/mo

**Goal:** Stop manually emailing. Start marketing.

- Automated branded confirmation emails when someone submits a request
- Track where leads come from (Google, Instagram, direct)
- Launch @venue.miami Instagram — 3 posts/week (venue spotlights, event inspo)
- Bilingual content (English/Spanish for Miami market)

**Cost:** $0 (free tools: Resend for email, Later for scheduling, Canva for design)

**Frank's role:** Help pick which venues to spotlight on Instagram. Provide feedback on messaging.

---

### Phase 3 — First Revenue (Month 2–3) 💰 $100–$500/mo

**Goal:** Close the first paying venue partner.

- Reach out to 30 venues with proof: "We sent you X leads last month for free"
- Offer revenue tiers:

| Tier | What they get | Price |
|------|--------------|-------|
| Free listing | We send leads, they respond within 24h | $0 |
| Featured placement | Top of results + "Featured" badge | $99/month |
| Commission | We get 10–15% on confirmed bookings | Per-booking |

- Add a `/partners` page on the site — pitch to venue owners
- A/B test the booking wizard to improve conversion

**Cost:** $0 (same tools)

**Frank's role:** Help with venue outreach. Warm intros to any Miami venue contacts. Review the partner pitch.

---

### Phase 4 — Real Platform (Month 3–6) 💰 $500–$2,000/mo

**Goal:** Database backend, venue partner dashboard, payment processing.

- Move from static data to a real database (venues + bookings + users)
- Venue partners get a dashboard: see their leads, update their listing, manage availability
- Payment processing for commission-based deals
- 50–100 monthly booking requests

**Cost:** ~$25/month (database hosting)

**Frank's role:** Strategic input on pricing tiers. Help negotiate commission rates with venues.

---

### Phase 5 — Scale (Month 6–12) 💰 $3,000–$8,000/mo

**Goal:** Expand venue count, add revenue streams, consider paid marketing.

- Expand to 100+ venues (new neighborhoods: Edgewater, Midtown, Key Biscayne, Doral, Aventura)
- Add-on services marketplace (photographer, florist, DJ referrals — $50–$100 per referral)
- Blog/content SEO for long-tail keywords
- Paid marketing: Instagram ads + Google Ads (only if organic works first)
- 200+ monthly booking requests, 30+ venue partners

**Cost:** ~$25/month + ad spend (TBD based on results)

## Revenue Model

| When | How we make money | Per unit | Monthly estimate |
|------|------------------|----------|-----------------|
| Month 1–2 | Free (proving demand) | $0 | $0 |
| Month 2–3 | Lead fees to venues | $25–$50 per lead | $100–$500 |
| Month 3–4 | + Featured placement | $99/month per venue | $500–$1,500 |
| Month 4–6 | + Booking commission | 10–15% of event | $1,000–$3,000 |
| Month 6–12 | + Add-on referrals | $50–$100 each | $3,000–$8,000 |

**Why commission wins long-term:** Average Miami private event = $2,500–$5,000. At 12% commission = $300–$600 per booking. 10 bookings/month = $3,000–$6,000/month.
