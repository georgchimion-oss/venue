## What Is VENUE?

VENUE is a free platform that helps people in Miami find and book private event spaces — birthday parties, corporate events, baby showers, weddings, and more. Users describe their event, and we match them with the right venue from our curated database of real Miami spaces.

**Think of it as:** The OpenTable of private events for Miami.

---

## The Problem

Planning a private event in Miami is frustrating:

- **Peerspace** — The market leader, but rated 2.6/5 stars on PissedConsumer. Complaints: impossible to reach customer service, refund nightmares, inconsistent venue quality. Charges venues 20% + guests 12-18%. No local touch.
- **Giggster** — Better reviewed, but focused on film/photo shoots, not private events. 962 venues listed in Miami but geared toward creatives, not birthday parties.
- **Event planners** — Charge $2,000–$10,000 just to find a space
- **Google/Yelp** — Hours of research, no way to filter by event type, guest count, or budget

**Nobody owns the "find a private event space in Miami" search — and the current options are either impersonal, expensive, or poorly reviewed.**

## How It Works

```
Google/Instagram Ad → User lands on VENUE → Booking wizard → Lead submitted → We connect user + venue → Venue pays us
```

1. **Paid ads drive traffic** — Google Ads ("birthday venue miami") + Instagram Ads (targeted at Miami women 25-45 planning events)
2. **User completes booking wizard** — picks event type, guest count, budget, vibe
3. **We show matched venues** — real Miami spaces with pricing, capacity, amenities
4. **User requests a space** — automated lead capture
5. **We connect user and venue** — automated emails, concierge follow-up
6. **Venue pays us** — commission on confirmed bookings or monthly placement fee

**Free for consumers. Always.** Venues pay because we send them qualified leads.
